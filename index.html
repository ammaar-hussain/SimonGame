Press Spacebar to Start

How to Play :

  1. Watch and listen to the
    sequence of colors and/or
    sounds played by the game.


  2. Repeat the sequence by
    clicking or tapping on the buttons
    in the same order.


  3. If you make a mistake
    or forget the sequence,
    the game ends.


  4. Try to beat your high score
    by completing as many rounds
    as possible without
    making a mistake.


  5. Enjoy challenging your
    memory and reflexes
    while having fun!
